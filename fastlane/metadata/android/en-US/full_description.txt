<p>This is a colorful shooting balloon game for entertainment.</p>
<p>At the beginning of the game there are not many colored balloons and they are revealed level by level.</p>
<p>After each level the balloons become a bit faster, their overall count is increasing, and the space they are coming from is getting bigger.</p>

<p><br><b>Playing instructions:</b></p>
<ol>
<li>The goal is to not allow all the coming pied Balloons to get into the center of the screen, where the crossbow is shooting with arrows.</li>
<li>The arrows are fired one after the other from the center and their direction is where your finger is touching the screen at the moment of firing.</li>
<li>When you crack up enough balloons, you go to the next level.</li>
</ol>

<p><br><b>Features:</b></p>
<ol>
<li>Increasing difficulty in levels.</li>
<li>7 unique background themes - 1 fortress, 3 brick walls and 3 gradients.</li>
<li>High scores - keeps track of your best result.</li>
<li>Supports different color palettes for different tastes.</li>
</ol>

<p><br><b>Permissions:</b></p>
<p>The app uses ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE and INTERNET permsisions, because it shows home ads and they open the default browser on the device, going to one of our other apps' page on the store or on the website of Metatrans Apps.
To be more precise, if the other app is available on the store, from which the app is downloaded, then opens the app page of the other app on the same store. If still the app is not available on the store, then opens the app page on our website.
The app pages on our website have app demo video on YouTube, so a connection to youtube domain will also be listed in order to visualize the page in the browser.
Our website is created by WordPress, so connections to other domains could be opened as well for technical purposes (e.g. downloading text fonts css).</p>
